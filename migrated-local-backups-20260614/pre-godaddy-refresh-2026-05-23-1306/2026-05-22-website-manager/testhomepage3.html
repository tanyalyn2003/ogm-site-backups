FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

The homepage should answer the essentials clearly: materials, service area, fabrication, installation, and what the consultation process actually looks like.

What countertop materials do you offer?

Granite, quartz, marble, quartzite, and custom glass solutions for kitchens, bathrooms, bars, and interior spaces.

Do you help with material selection?

Yes. The team helps compare materials, finishes, and design direction based on the look and function the space needs.

Do you provide custom fabrication and installation?

Yes. Every project is measured, fabricated, and installed to fit the exact layout and detailing requirements of the room.

Do you install custom shower glass?

Yes. Olive Glass & Marble installs frameless shower enclosures, mirrors, and other custom glass features for elevated interiors.

Do you work outside Fayetteville?

Yes. The company serves Fayetteville, Pinehurst, Southern Pines, Hope Mills, the Fort Liberty area, and nearby North Carolina communities.

Can I request an estimate for my project?

Yes. Estimates are available as part of the consultation process and are based on project scope, selected materials, and installation details.
