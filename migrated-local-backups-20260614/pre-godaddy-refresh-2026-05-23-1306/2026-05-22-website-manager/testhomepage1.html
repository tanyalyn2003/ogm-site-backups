FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What countertop materials do you offer?

We offer granite, quartz, marble, quartzite, and custom glass solutions for kitchens, bathrooms, bars, and interior spaces.

Do you help with material selection?

Yes. We help clients compare materials, finishes, and design direction based on the look and function they want in the space.

Do you provide custom fabrication and installation?

Yes. Every project is measured, fabricated, and installed to fit the exact layout and detailing requirements of the room.

Do you install custom shower glass?

Yes. We install frameless shower enclosures, mirrors, and other custom glass features for elevated interior spaces.

Do you work outside Fayetteville?

Yes. We serve Fayetteville, Pinehurst, Southern Pines, Hope Mills, the Fort Liberty area, and nearby North Carolina communities.

Can I request an estimate for my project?

Yes. Estimates are available during the consultation process based on project scope, material selection, and installation details.
