Community Standards & HR Guidelines
This board is a company-provided resource. All activity is subject to the Employee Code of Conduct.
What’s allowed
  • Personal items for sale or giveaway (furniture, electronics, clothing, household goods)
  • Personal services offered by employees (tutoring, photography, handyman, pet care, etc.)
  • Help wanted posts for personal tasks (moving, yardwork, childcare, etc.)
  • Event tickets and community opportunities
What’s not allowed
  • Commercial business solicitation, multi-level marketing, or pyramid schemes
  • Weapons, ammunition, controlled substances, or any illegal items
  • Adult content, offensive materials, or anything that violates the Employee Code of Conduct
  • Political campaigning, fundraising, or religious solicitation
  • Company property, assets, or proprietary materials
  • Items or services that create a conflict of interest with your role
Your responsibilities
  • All transactions are strictly between employees. The company is not a party to any sale or agreement and assumes no liability.
  • Participation is voluntary. No employee may feel pressured to buy, sell, or engage with any listing.
  • Disputes between employees are personal matters. HR will not mediate private transactions but may take action if conduct violations occur.
  • Listings may be removed by HR or administrators at any time without notice if they violate this policy.
  • Posting a listing constitutes your agreement that the content complies with this policy and the Employee Code of Conduct.
Questions? Contact HR at hr@company.com · To report a violation, use the Report button on any listing or email HR directly. · Last updated: January 2025

Employee Peer-to-Peer Exchange Policy

Effective: January 1, 2025  ·  Maintained by: Human Resources

Purpose
The Team Marketplace is a voluntary, employee-operated notice board provided as a convenience. It allows employees to buy, sell, give away personal items, offer personal services, and request help from colleagues. This policy establishes the rules governing appropriate use.

Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and interns who access the Team Marketplace, regardless of location or employment classification.

Permitted use
Employees may post personal items for sale or giveaway, personal services (tutoring, photography, home repair, etc.), help-wanted requests for personal tasks, and community event information.

Prohibited use
The following are strictly prohibited: commercial or business advertising; multi-level marketing or pyramid scheme promotion; weapons, controlled substances, or illegal goods; adult or offensive content; political or religious solicitation; company assets or proprietary information; any content that creates a conflict of interest.

No company liability
The company provides this platform as a courtesy only. It does not endorse, guarantee, or assume any responsibility for items, services, or transactions. All transactions are solely between individual employees.

No coercion
Participation is entirely voluntary. No employee, supervisor, or manager may pressure another employee to buy, sell, or respond to any listing. Violations may be reported to HR.

Equal opportunity & non-discrimination
All use of the marketplace must comply with the company’s Equal Employment Opportunity and Anti-Harassment policies. Content that discriminates, demeans, or targets individuals based on any protected characteristic is prohibited and may result in disciplinary action.

Privacy
Contact information posted is visible to other employees. Employees who choose to post anonymously do so with the understanding that HR and platform administrators retain access to their identity for policy enforcement purposes only.

Enforcement
HR reserves the right to remove any listing at any time without notice. Violations may result in loss of marketplace access and disciplinary action up to and including termination, consistent with the Employee Code of Conduct.

Reporting
Concerns about listings may be reported using the Report button on any card or by emailing HR at hr@company.com. All reports are treated confidentially.

Questions about this policy should be directed to Human Resources.
