Countertops in Real Homes

Explore our curated gallery of custom granite, quartz, marble, and glass installations — beautifully crafted for kitchens, bathrooms, fireplaces, and outdoor spaces throughout Fayetteville, Pinehurst, and greater North Carolina.

At Olive Glass & Marble, we combine expert craftsmanship with premium materials to bring elegant stone and glass surfaces into your home. Whether you are building a modern kitchen, upgrading a traditional bathroom, or designing a custom outdoor bar, our team ensures each project is built to last and designed to inspire.

Based in Fayetteville, NC, we proudly serve homeowners, contractors, and designers within a 60-mile radius — including Pinehurst, Southern Pines, Sanford, Lumberton, and beyond. Every image you see below reflects real work completed by our team in real homes across North Carolina.
