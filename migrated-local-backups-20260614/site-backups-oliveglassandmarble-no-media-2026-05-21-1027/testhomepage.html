Our Process

A Refined Process Built Around Precision

Begin with a design-focused consultation, move through precise templating and fabrication, and finish with installation handled by a team that values clean detailing and long-term performance. The process is tailored to your space, your material choice, and the finished look you want the room to carry.

01 · Consultation

Material selection & planning

Start with a conversation around your space, aesthetic preferences, and functional goals. We help narrow materials, edge details, sink options, and finishing selections so the direction is clear before fabrication begins.

  • Granite, marble, quartz & more
  • Edge profiles and finishing options
  • Sink and appliance coordination
02 · Fabrication

CNC-cut, hand-finished

We use digital templating, precise CNC cutting, and hand finishing to ensure every seam, edge, and polished surface reflects the quality expected in a premium installation.

  • Digital ProLiner templating
  • Precision CNC cutting
  • Hand-polished finishes
03 · Installation

Clean, on-time, respectful

Our in-house crews protect the home, set every surface carefully, and leave the finished space polished, clean, and ready to live in. The result is a precise fit and a professional finish that feels intentional from every angle.

  • Old countertop removal & disposal
  • Final sealing & polish
  • Detailed walk-through inspection
Material Options

Premium Stones for Every Style

From bold statement pieces to timeless classics, we offer a wide selection of materials to match any design vision. Choose from full slabs or cost-effective remnant pieces — perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, bars, and more.

Our curated inventory includes granite, marble, quartz, and quartzite, along with a selection of specialty stones. Whether you are drawn to the natural movement of granite, the soft elegance of marble, or the low-maintenance appeal of engineered quartz, we will help you find the ideal surface for your space.

Our expert team is here to guide you through material comparisons, edge styles, and finish options — ensuring the stone you select aligns with your aesthetic, functionality needs, and budget.

Granite — Bianco Antico

A durable and timeless choice with unique patterning and natural speckling. Granite is heat-resistant and ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and outdoor use. Bianco Antico blends well in both traditional and transitional styles.

Natural Stone
Explore Granite

Quartzite — Taj Mahal

A natural stone known for its elegant veining and creamy tones. Quartzite is harder than granite, offering superior resistance to heat, scratches, and daily wear — perfect for high-end kitchens and bathrooms.

Natural Stone
Explore Quartzite

Marble — Carrara White

Classic and refined, Carrara marble brings timeless beauty with soft veining and cool tones. Ideal for vanities, islands, and feature walls where elegance and character are key. Requires sealing and care to preserve its finish.

Natural Stone
Explore Marble

Quartz — Calacatta Miraggio Gold

A premium engineered stone designed for uniformity and easy upkeep. Featuring bold veining and warm undertones, it is perfect for modern kitchens, baths, and spaces where durability meets sophistication.

Frequently Asked Questions

What countertop materials do you offer?
