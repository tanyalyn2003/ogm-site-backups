Fabrication & Installation

Custom glass fabrication and installation in Fayetteville, NC for showers, mirrors, tabletops, and cut-to-spec projects.

Olive Glass & Marble handles field measure, hardware coordination, finish review, shop fabrication, and final installation with one local team so homeowners, builders, and designers are not piecing together separate vendors for the same glass project.

Field Measure

Shower openings, mirror walls, and specialty pieces measured for the real conditions on site.

We verify the opening, check wall conditions, confirm hardware locations, and account for finished surfaces before glass is ordered or fabricated so the final fit is based on the room you actually have.

Shop Preparation

Glass type, edge finish, thickness, and hardware details reviewed before fabrication.

From frameless shower layouts to custom mirrors and tabletop glass, we confirm the practical details first so the shop work supports the design instead of forcing compromises at install time.

Installation Day

One local team carrying the project from final layout to finished placement.

Install work is coordinated around access, finished tile, hardware finishes, and final alignment so the glass looks intentional, operates correctly, and leaves the space ready to use.

If you are comparing layouts or specialty pieces, review our glass process, browse recent glass projects, and request a custom quote once the space is ready for measure.

Fayetteville FAQs

Answers to the questions we hear before the final field measure.

FAQ 1

When should we schedule the final shower glass measurement?

Schedule the final measure after tile, waterproofing, and framing details are complete. That gives us the exact opening dimensions needed for a clean fit.

FAQ 2

Can you match hardware finishes and coordinate with our contractor?

Yes. We regularly coordinate hardware finishes, hinge choices, and installation timing with contractors, designers, and homeowners so the glass feels integrated with the rest of the room.

FAQ 3

Do you only handle showers?

No. We also fabricate and install mirrors, bath enclosures, shelves, tabletop glass, and specialty cut glass for residential and commercial projects.
